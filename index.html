Privacy Policy for 6.1

Last updated: June 10, 2026

6.1 (“the app,” “we,” “us”) is a fitness tracking app for logging body weight, workouts, and nutrition. The app is operated by Hassan Al-Ghanim (“the developer”). This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at hassanbmag@gmail.com.

Information we collect

Account information. When you create an account, we collect your email address and a securely hashed password. If you sign in with Apple instead, we receive the account identifier Apple provides, and — only if you choose to share it — your name and email address.

Fitness data you enter. The information you log in the app, including body weight entries, workouts (exercises, sets, reps, weights, and your training split), food and macronutrient entries, and your personal goals. You decide what to enter.

Subscription information. 6.1 offers a paid subscription. Your payment is processed entirely by Apple — we never receive or store your payment card details. We use RevenueCat to verify and manage your subscription status; RevenueCat processes an anonymous app user identifier and your purchase and receipt data.

Limited technical data. Basic information required to connect your device to our backend and keep your account secure. We do not use third-party advertising trackers or analytics that profile you across other apps or websites.

How we use your information

  • To provide the app’s core features — recording and displaying your fitness data.
  • To sync your data across your devices and back it up so you don’t lose it.
  • To create, maintain, and secure your account.
  • To manage your subscription and your access to paid features.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising.

Where your data is stored

Your account and fitness data are stored using our backend provider, Supabase, on servers located in the European Union (Frankfurt, Germany). Data is transmitted over encrypted connections. The app also keeps a copy of your data on your device so it works offline, and syncs that copy to the server when you reconnect.

Who we share data with

We share data only with the service providers needed to run 6.1:

  • Supabase — database hosting and account authentication.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management.
  • Apple — account sign-in (Sign in with Apple) and payment processing.

These providers process data on our behalf under their own privacy and security terms. We do not sell your data or share it with advertisers.

Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data and fitness logs are removed from our systems within 30 days.

Your rights and choices

  • View and edit your data at any time within the app.
  • Delete individual entries within the app.
  • Request deletion of your entire account and all associated data by emailing us at hassanbmag@gmail.com.

Because your data is stored in the European Union, you may also have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these, contact us at the email above.

Security

Each user’s data is isolated so that you can only access your own (enforced at the database level). Data is encrypted while in transit. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

Children’s privacy

6.1 is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under that age. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves — for example, when new features are added. We will post the updated version on this page with a new “last updated” date.
